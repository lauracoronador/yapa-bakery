A little extra, made with everything.

In Bolivia, yapa means a small gift given freely — a little something extra the vendor adds to your bag with a smile. That spirit is baked into everything we make.

From our home kitchen in Santa Clara County, we craft traditional Bolivian salteñas alongside homemade cakes, cookies, and sweets — each one made to order in small batches so nothing goes to waste and everything arrives fresh.
